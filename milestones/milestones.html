Randactyl's First Veteran Maelstrom Arena Clear

August 30th, 2016

After almost a month of painstaking practice, Randactyl finally cleared Veteran Maelstrom Arena - the most challenging piece of solo content in The Elder Scrolls Online. The win came on his stamina sorcerer, Stuffums, the largest Orc that can possibly be created. This came as quite the surprise to those who knew Stuffums when he first ventured into Tamriel; a bright-eyed, frost staff wielding, amateur chef.
